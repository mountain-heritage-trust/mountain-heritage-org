Doug Scott CBE (1941–2020) was one of the leading mountaineers of his generation. He served as President of the Alpine Club and Vice-President of the BMC, and represented the UK on the UIAA, the world body for mountaineering. As a former member of the UIAA Management Committee, his aim was to help preserve the best traditions of mountaineering. He considered the Mountain Heritage Trust, where he was a Trustee, vital in informing the current generation of climbers about the great traditions of the sport. He was patron of, and an active supporter of a range of charities aimed at providing health and education to Nepalese communities and to improving the welfare and working conditions for the porters in Nepal.
