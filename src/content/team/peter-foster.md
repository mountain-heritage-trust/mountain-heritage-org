Peter has been interested in the history of mountaineering since his school days. After retiring as a consultant physician, he has written numerous articles for the *Alpine Journal* and a biography of the physiologist and mountaineer Thomas Graham Brown, *The Uncrowned King of Mont Blanc*, which was shortlisted for the Boardman Tasker Prize in 2020. He is a member of the Alpine Club and still climbs and skis in the Alps most years.
