Rachael is a qualified Parish Clerk and AAT-qualified accountant. She lives in Penrith with her husband and two children, and works part-time for the Mountain Heritage Trust alongside roles as a Town and Parish Development Officer, Parish Council Clerk and Senior Accounts Coordinator. Although born in Kent, she moved to Threlkeld in 1995, where her maternal family goes back generations. A keen walker and a committed mum, Rachael's administrative skills support the Trust's Blencathra office operations.
