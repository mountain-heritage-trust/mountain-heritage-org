Last month MHT and Community Action Nepal hosted a joint fund raising event with Sir Chris Bonington, Ian Parnell and compère Julie Summers. The night was a great success, we raised around £20,000 between the two charities from ticket sales, auction items and our raffle.

We would like to thank our sponsors for kindly donating prizes for our raffle:

G.H.Mumm Champagne  
Berghaus  
National Trust  
British Mountaineering Council

We would also like to thank our speakers Chris and Ian and fantastic compère Julie, and also everyone who attended the evening, thank you for making it such a success
