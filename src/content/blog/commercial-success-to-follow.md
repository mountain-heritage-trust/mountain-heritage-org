Plans are underway to develop Dan Smith’s exceedingly wearable and fully functional “Everywhere” Jacket for the commercial market. The design, which draws inspiration from the jacket George Mallory wore on the 1924 Mount Everest expedition, is being developed under the Northborn Ltd brand.  It’s appeal is shown in the recent [article](http://www.mountain-heritage.org/cms/wp-content/uploads/Trail-SEP12-Mallory-jacket.pdf)which appeared in the September issue of Trail.
