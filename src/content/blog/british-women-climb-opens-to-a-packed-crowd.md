From pioneering travellers, to bold adventurers and elite athletes; the exhibition focuses on the experiences of women climbers, their challenges to social expectations, and their mountaineering achievements across history.

The exhibition includes an exciting range of historic artefacts: Lake District icon Mabel Barker’s rope and boots, original Ladies’ [Alpine Club](http://www.alpine-club.org.uk/)artefacts, and loans from the [Fell and Rock Climbing Club](http://www.frcc.co.uk/), the [Pinnacle Club](http://www.pinnacleclub.co.uk/) and world bouldering champion [Shauna Coxsey](http://www.shaunacoxsey.co.uk/).
