You can now support the Mountain Heritage Trust when you shop with amazon through the amazon smile programme.

Just shop through smile.amazon.co.uk, select [Mountain Heritage Trust](https://smile.amazon.co.uk/ch/1083219-0) as your chosen charity to support, and we then receive 0.5% of all eligible purchases at no extra cost to you.

Thank you for your support, all funds are used to support the work of the Mountain Heritage Trust in preserving and sharing Britain's mountain and climbing history.
