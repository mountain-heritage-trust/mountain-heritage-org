Mountaineer, adventurer, author and businessman Kelvin Kent, returns to Jersey on Friday 13th September to deliver an Illustrated Talk about the spirit of human endeavour and life on the mountain. Kent will be taking a look at two historic and classic Himalayan Climbs of over 40 years ago: The South Face of Annapurna & the Southwest Face of Everest contrasting pre and post Monsoon mountaineering expeditions in Nepal. Eric Blakeley MBE, an acquaintance of Kelvin and fellow climber, will host a Q&A.
