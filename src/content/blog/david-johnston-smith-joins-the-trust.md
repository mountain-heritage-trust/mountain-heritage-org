July 4th, 2022 was a good day for the Mountain Heritage Trust when David Johnston-Smith joined the Trust at the Blencathra Office.  David has brought skills and experience to the Trust and a new perspective in managing our important collections.  Ian Smith (MHT Vice Chair) welcomed David to the MHT office.
