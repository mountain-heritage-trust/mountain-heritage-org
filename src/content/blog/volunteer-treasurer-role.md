The Mountain Heritage Trust (MHT), is a non-profit charity established in 2000 to record and preserve Britain’s rich heritage in the fields of climbing, mountaineering and mountain culture. Second to none in terms of breadth, Britain has a proud legacy in the Alps, the Himalaya, and the other great mountain ranges as well as closer to home on British rock.

Collections of historic items from Britain’s rich mountaineering heritage have been preserved and archived to create an innovative and exciting heritage offer for local, national, and international audiences. Researchers have also been welcomed to come and inspect the collections for their own investigations.

The Trust is entering a new phase of development over the next three years and we are looking for a Treasurer to help guide and advise us on the financial health of the Trust. The Treasurer will work closely with the Finance & Administrative Assistant (currently being recruited), who will be responsible for the day-to-day finances of the Trust, which will enable the Treasurer to present a monthly cash flow projection in line with budgetary controls.

We are looking for a Volunteer Treasurer who is keen to be a part of an exciting charity.

For more details please see [the role description here](https://drive.google.com/file/d/11_fh3ZguhY4_oiQxV9m4b_4JO5-DjGZX/view?usp=sharing).

Please email enquiries@mountain-heritage.org for a full role profile and details on how to apply.
