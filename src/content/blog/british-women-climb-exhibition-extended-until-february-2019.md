Included in the exhibition is a range of historic artefacts: Lake District icon Mabel Barker’s rope and boots, original Ladies’ [Alpine Club](http://www.alpine-club.org.uk/) artefacts, and loans from the [Fell and Rock Climbing Club](http://www.frcc.co.uk/), the [Pinnacle Club](http://www.pinnacleclub.co.uk/) as well as world bouldering champion [Shauna Coxsey](http://www.shaunacoxsey.co.uk/)‘s shoes and championship medal.

The exhibition is installed at [Keswick Museum](http://www.keswickmuseum.org.uk/), for which access information and opening times can be found [here](https://keswickmuseum.org.uk/visit/).
