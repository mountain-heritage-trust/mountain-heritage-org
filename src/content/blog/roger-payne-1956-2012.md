A memorial service to celebrate the life of Roger Payne who was killed in the avalanche on Mont Maudit on 12th July will be held at Manchester Town Hall on Saturday 8th September. The event will start at 6.30pm and is generously supported by the British Mountaineering Council.  Further information and directions are available [here](http://www.thebmc.co.uk/celebrating-the-life-of-roger-payne).

Our condolences go to Julie-Ann and his very many friends in the world of climbing and mountaineering.

A full tribute to Roger from the BMC and links to related articles can be found [here](http://www.thebmc.co.uk/roger-payne-killed-on-mont-maudit-ed-douglas-looks-back-on-his-life)
