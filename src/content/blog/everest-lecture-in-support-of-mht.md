This September, Mountain Heritage Trust Chair Julie Summers will give an illustrated lecture on the infamous and romantic figure of Everest mountaineer Andrew Irvine in support of MHT. This is the third instalment of the very popular annual Longlands event hosted by trustee Jeff Ford to raise funds for the trust.

The lecture will be held on the evening of Wednesday the 24th of September, starting at 7.30pm.

Tickets are £18 each, and includes a welcome drink and canapés.
