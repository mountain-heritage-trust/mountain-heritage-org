There will be lots of opportunities for a wide range of people to get involved with this project, through partner organisations, attending the exhibition and events, or as a volunteer. We’ll be recruiting volunteers to collect oral history interviews – a great chance to hear first hand some of the unwritten histories of the people, places and organisations that Chris has had an influence on – and for exhibition research and curation alongside professional curators. An advert with more details about volunteering will be available onthe Mountain Heritage Trust website soon.
