Dan Smith’s project to create a new jacket based on that worn by George Mallory was unveiled last night.  He has fashioned a very wearable and functional piece which is currently on display at the REVEAL Graduate Show at Northumbria University from 21st – 29th June and at The Design Centre, London 4-7 July, together with the Mallory Replica garment.
