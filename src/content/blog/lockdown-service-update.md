Following government guidance, the Mountain Heritage Trust have advised their staff to work from home commencing Thursday 5th November.

As a result, the Trust's office will remain closed to researchers until further notice and our remote enquiry service will be very limited. We will still be responding to emails but will not have telephone access.

We will be making regular checks on the collections to ensure their safety and security, but will not be accessing them for researchers or enquiries until we resume work from our office base.

Thank you for your patience and understanding, we will issue updates via our website and social media channels.
