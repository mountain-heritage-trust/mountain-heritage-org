MHT will be putting a small selection of items from its collections on display at Keswick Museum this Saturday to complement the Keswick Mountain Festival.  The Museum will also be hosting a book fair and MHT will have its own stall selling copies of Simon Yates’ latest book as well as copies of the Abraham Brothers photographic calendar of Skye for 2013. There will also be back copies of various mountain magazines available to help you plug those gaps. Further details on the book fair and book signings by Simon Yates here.
