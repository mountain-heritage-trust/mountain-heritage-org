Peter Habeler, Doug, Chris and Leo had kindly signed a WWII ice axe donated to MHT which raised £600 on the evening. The vintage axe was purchased by Berghaus for display in their reception area, so many thanks to [Berghaus](http://store.berghaus.com/)! An original water colour painting of Pillar Rock, also generously donated, raised £500. The two items were generously given by local people who support both charities and remarked how delighted they were with the outcome. The total achieved from the auction for MHT was £687.50 after reclaiming gift aid.
