We're delighted to report that the Mountain Heritage Trust has been accepted onto the Heritage Compass business support programme.

Led by [Cause4](https://www.cause4.co.uk/) working with [Creative United](https://www.creativeunited.org.uk/) and the [Arts Marketing Association](https://www.a-m-a.co.uk/), we'll be joining a cohort of 24 other small and medium cultural organisations to participate in this [National Lottery Heritage Fund](https://www.heritagefund.org.uk/) funded programme.

"This new programme is designed to allow a wide range of heritage organisations to build their resilience through working together, as well as developing their individual strategies. Organisations will gain tailored training, mentoring and peer learning, developing strategy and moving forward as part of a dynamic new cohort of Heritage organisations." (Cause4, 2020)

We can't wait to get started!
