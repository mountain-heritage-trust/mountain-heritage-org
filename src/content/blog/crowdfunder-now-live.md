The Mountain Heritage Trust needs your help to secure the future of this internationally important archive of climbing and mountaineering.

It has been 25 years since the MHT was founded by Chris Bonnington, John Innerdale, Lord Roger Chorley, Audrey Salkeld, James Smith and John Porter to protect, share and help people engage with the UK’s rich climbing and mountaineering heritage.

Throughout our history we’ve relied on a wide variety of funding sources, from commercial sponsors to grants, and to celebrate MHT’s 25th birthday we are looking to crowdfund £25,000 to cover the cost of maintaining and future proofing the archive.

Your support will help maintain our climate controlled archive space, partially fund a collections manager and get the ball rolling on digitising some of the incredible slides that exist in our collection.
