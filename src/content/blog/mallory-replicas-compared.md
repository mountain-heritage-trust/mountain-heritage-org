It was a real pleasure to meet with Geordie Stewart yesterday, the youngest Brit to climb the Seven Summits. MHT were assisting in filming a segment for an upcoming film which compares the clothing worn by Geordie to that worn by George Mallory. Geordie was impressed with the level of innovation apparent in 1924 as well as the skill and knowledge of the Replica team.  A brochure which details the replica project in full is available from MHT priced £4.95.
