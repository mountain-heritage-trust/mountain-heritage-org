The next MHT fundraising lecture sees Andy Houseman giving an illustrated talk about his experiences on the South Face of Denali and the North Face of Chamlang.  The date of the event is 31st January and start time is 7pm at the Rheged Centre, Penrith, Cumbria.   Tickets are priced £15 for adults and £10 for children and concessions.
