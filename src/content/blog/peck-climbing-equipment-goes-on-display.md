Thanks to a recent donation from Derek Gamble Managing Director of Peck Climbing Equipment, a variety of items including crackers, chocks, peg-pitons, bandoliers and the noted MacInnes Terrordactyl is currently on display at Charlotte Mason Library, University of Cumbria, Ambleside.  Excerpts from the catalogue of 1968 provide detailed information on the development of these items for which John Cleare supplied the photographs. A snapshot of the display is available [here.](http://www.mountain-heritage.org/cms/wp-content/uploads/Peck-Exh1.jpg)
