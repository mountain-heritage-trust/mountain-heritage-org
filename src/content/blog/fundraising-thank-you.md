A huge thank you to [The Climbers' Club](https://www.climbers-club.co.uk/) who donated £425 from the raffle at their annual dinner to the Mountain Heritage Trust. Raffle proceeds were also donated to [Llanberis Mountain Rescue Team](http://www.llanberismountainrescue.co.uk/) and we hear a great evening was had by all.

Founded in 1898 The Climbers' Club is a national club for committed climbers; they aim to encourage mountaineering and rock climbing as well as promoting the interests of mountaineers and the mountain environment. The club has an impressive historic archive, much of which is stored at [Caernarfon Record Office](https://www.gwynedd.llyw.cymru/en/Residents/Libraries-and-archives/Archives-and-family-history/Archives-and-family-history.aspx).

If you'd like to support us in our work to preserve and share British mountain heritage you can make a regular or one off donation at our Just Giving page:
