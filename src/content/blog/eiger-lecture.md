MHT is hosting a fundraising lecture given by Sir Chris Bonington and Ueli Steck about their exploits entitled “Triumph and Tragedy on the Eiger”at the RGS on Thursday 1st December at 7pm.  Sir Chris will talk about his first ascent in 1966 and Ueli will expand on his incredible solo climb in 2008.
