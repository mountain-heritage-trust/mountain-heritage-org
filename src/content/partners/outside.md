Outside is an independent outdoor retailer based in Hathersage, in the heart of the Peak District, supplying clothing and equipment for climbing, walking and mountaineering.

Outside supports Mountain Heritage Trust in its work to preserve and share the heritage of British climbing and mountaineering.
