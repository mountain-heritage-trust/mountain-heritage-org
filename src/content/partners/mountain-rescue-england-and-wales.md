Mountain Rescue England and Wales is the representative body for the volunteer teams who provide search and rescue across the mountains, moorlands and rural areas of England and Wales.

Mountain Heritage Trust works with Mountain Rescue England and Wales to record and celebrate the history of mountain rescue and the people who have given their time to it.
