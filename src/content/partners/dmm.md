DMM is a British designer and manufacturer of climbing, mountaineering and industrial safety equipment, made at its factory in Llanberis in the heart of Snowdonia.

DMM supports Mountain Heritage Trust in its work to preserve and share the heritage of British climbing and mountaineering.
