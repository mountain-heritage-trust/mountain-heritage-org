The British Mountaineering Council (BMC) is the representative body that promotes the interests of climbers, hill walkers and mountaineers in England and Wales.

Mountain Heritage Trust works closely with the BMC to preserve and share the heritage of British climbing and mountaineering, and to bring the stories held in our collections to a wider audience.
