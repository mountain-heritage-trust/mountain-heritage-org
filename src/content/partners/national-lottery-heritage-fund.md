The National Lottery Heritage Fund is the largest dedicated funder of heritage in the UK, distributing money raised by National Lottery players to projects that sustain and transform the nation's heritage.

Thanks to National Lottery players, the Fund has supported Mountain Heritage Trust's work to catalogue, conserve and care for its collections so that they can be enjoyed for generations to come.
