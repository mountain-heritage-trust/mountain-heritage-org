The Climbers Shop was opened by Frank Davies in 1959 as the first outdoor shop in Ambleside, and is now the oldest independently owned outdoor clothing and equipment shop in the Lake District and one of the longest established in the UK. From its home at Compston Corner it has spent more than sixty years supplying climbers, mountaineers, hill walkers and fell runners — and outfitting expeditions around the world — with a deep knowledge of the gear and the ground it is used on.

Its long history is woven into the story of British mountaineering, which is why we are proud to count The Climbers Shop among the supporters of Mountain Heritage Trust as we work to preserve and celebrate that shared heritage.
